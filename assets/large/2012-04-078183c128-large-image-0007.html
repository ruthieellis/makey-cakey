Tea Time Treats Blogging Challenge: April



Following last month's scrumptious scone round-up (with over 56 entires!!) by the brilliant Karen from Lavender and Lovage, April's Tea Time Treats theme is:

EASTER!

But of course! Yep, if you're baking up a storm this Easter don't forget to send in your super Simnel Cakes, your heavenly Hot Cross Buns, your crackin' chocolaty creations and your egg-cellent Easter treats!

As always, the guidelines for Tea Time Treats can be found here. All tasty types of treats, sweet and savoury, are most welcome and don't forget to email teatimetreats@yahoo.co.uk by 28th April 2012 to be included in the round-up! 

Happy Baking!
