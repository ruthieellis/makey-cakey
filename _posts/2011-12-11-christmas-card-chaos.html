I'm really pleased with how it turned out - but it belies the chaos that went into making it!



There was drawing all over the furniture, head first diving off the chair, climbing on the coffee table, tears and concilatory banana loaf!

Firstly, I let Mini-M go crazy with the felt tips on a piece of green card. We also stuck on some sticky stars. The look of concentration when she was trying to get the sticky star from her finger to the page was intense!
Pre chair-diving incident...
I then used this free template from RobertSabuda.com to turn it in to a Christmas Tree - as simple as that.
