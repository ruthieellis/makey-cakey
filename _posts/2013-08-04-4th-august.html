A cross section of the biggest red cabbage ever - I took this photo after I'd removed, washed and chopped at least half of the leaves. It was about half a metre high! And it made a very tasty sausage and cabbage casserole for some hungry and wet hillwalkers returning from a little jaunt along the West Highland Way.
