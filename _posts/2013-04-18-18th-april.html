Mini-M's artwork. This is of Sarah from 'Sarah and duck' which is a kids tv programme. She very carefully explained it all: the smaller 'circle' on the right is Sarah's head. The bigger circle all around is her body. The two small dots are her eyes. The two lines sticking out to the right are Sarah's arms and the lines at the bottom are "lots of legs". A little Picasso-esque...
