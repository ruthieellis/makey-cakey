--- title: Eggy Bread Fruit Cake date: '2011-10-05T22:18:00.000+01:00' author: Ruth Ellis tags: - cakey - recipes - breakfast - sweet modified_time: '2011-10-05T22:18:00.178+01:00' thumbnail: http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-7Dv2Q43qBXs/Tot3m1Sdx5I/AAAAAAAABag/E9ik5sHgyw4/s72-c/101_0364.JPG blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-1766292537874588855.post-7139288623665963874 blogger_orig_url: https://makey-cakey.blogspot.com/2011/10/eggy-bread-fruit-cake.html --- If you are looking for a weekend breakfast treat, but prefer something sweet to the fry-up variety, then this might just fit the bill!

It's quick and easy, provided you have some fruitcake to hand, which we did - thanks Mum!



I simply beat up an egg in a shallow casserole dish, and cut some thin slices of fruit cake. I left them to soak in the beaten egg for a couple of minutes, turned them and left them for a couple more.

I then heated 1tbsp sunflower oil in a frying pan, and cooked the slices for 2-3 mins on each side over a low heat.


To serve I sprinkled over some icing sugar - delicious!
